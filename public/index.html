Frequently Asked Questions

What is remote notarization?

+

Remote notarization allows you to have documents notarized online via video conference, eliminating the need for in-person meetings. It's legally recognized and uses advanced security measures to verify your identity.

Can you notarize documents from any state?

+

Yes! We can notarize documents for clients located anywhere in the United States, as long as the document type is eligible for remote notarization in your state.

What do I need for a remote notarization?

+

You'll need a computer or mobile device with camera and microphone, a valid government-issued photo ID, and the document(s) to be notarized. We'll guide you through the entire process.

How long does the process take?

+

Most remote notarizations take 10-15 minutes. We offer same-day appointments and emergency services for urgent documents.

Is remote notarization legally binding?

+

Absolutely! Remote notarizations are legally equivalent to traditional in-person notarizations and are recognized by courts, banks, and government agencies.

What are your rates?

+

Our standard rate is $25 per notarization. We offer package deals for multiple documents and discounted rates for regular business clients. Contact us for a custom quote.
